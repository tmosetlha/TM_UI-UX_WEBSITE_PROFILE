About

Tshiamo Mosetlha

TM_UI/UX is the practice and the alter ego of Tshiamo Mosetlha — a developer working at the line between design and code. The name carries its own logic: TM for the person, UI/UX for the discipline, and the fire for how it gets made.

The philosophy is simple — born in fire, built in code. Every project starts as someone else's vision: a design file, a sketch, a brief. The work is taking that and turning it into something that actually runs — a website, a web app, an Android app — without losing what made the original design worth building.

No house style gets imposed on your project. The design you bring is the design that ships.
