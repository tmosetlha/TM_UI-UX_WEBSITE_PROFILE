Process

From design to build

A real sequence — each step needs the one before it finished, so the order here is the actual order of work.

  • 01

    Brief & design handoff

    You share your design — Figma, XD, images, or a clear written brief — along with what the product needs to do.

  • 02

    Scope & stack

    We confirm what's being built — website, web app, Android app — and the tools needed to do it properly.

  • 03

    Build

    Development happens in a structured, step-by-step way, with progress shared along the way rather than disappearing until the end.

  • 04

    Review

    You see the build against the original design, and any adjustments get made before anything ships.

  • 05

    Delivery

    The finished product is handed over — deployed, documented, and ready to use.
